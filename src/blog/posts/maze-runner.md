Maze Runner drops you into a procedurally generated maze. Your goal is to reach the exit as quickly as possible. A timer tracks your completion time so you can try to beat your personal best on each new layout.

## How it works

Each game generates a new random maze. You start at one corner and need to reach the opposite corner. The timer begins when the maze loads and stops when you reach the exit. There are no enemies or hazards — just you and the walls.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Arrow keys or WASD to move.
- **Mobile:** Swipe in the direction you want to move, or use on-screen directional buttons.

## Strategy/Tips

- Use the wall-following method: pick a wall (left or right) and keep your hand on it. This guarantees you reach the exit eventually.
- Look ahead before committing to a path. Dead ends cost time.
- On repeated plays, try to improve your pattern recognition rather than relying on trial and error.
