Pong is a recreation of the classic arcade game. You control one paddle while the CPU controls the other. A ball bounces between the two sides. Score by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle. First player to 5 points wins the match.

## How it works

The ball launches from the center and bounces off the top and bottom walls. Each paddle deflects the ball back toward the opposite side. The angle of deflection depends on where the ball hits the paddle. If the ball passes a paddle, the opposing player scores a point. The match resets to center after each point.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Move your paddle with the mouse or use the Up and Down arrow keys.
- **Mobile:** Drag your finger up and down on your side of the screen.

## Strategy/Tips

- Position your paddle to hit the ball with the edge rather than the center. This sends it at a sharper angle, making it harder for the CPU to return.
- Stay near the center of your side between shots. It gives you the best chance to react.
- Watch the ball's trajectory early and move to intercept rather than chasing it.
