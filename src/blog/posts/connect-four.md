Connect Four is a two-player game played on a vertical 7x6 grid. Players take turns dropping colored discs into columns. The first player to line up four discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

## How it works

Discs fall to the lowest available position in the selected column. If all 42 spaces fill up without four in a row, the game is a draw.

## Controls

Click or tap a column to drop your disc.

## Strategy

The center column is the strongest opening position — it connects to the most potential four-in-a-row combinations. Watch for situations where your opponent has two ways to win on their next turn (a "fork"), and block early. Building threats in multiple directions simultaneously is more effective than chasing a single line.
