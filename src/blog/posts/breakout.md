Breakout is an arcade game where you control a paddle at the bottom of the screen. A ball bounces around the play area, and your job is to keep it in play while it destroys bricks above.

## How it works

The ball launches from the paddle and ricochets off walls and bricks. Each brick hit removes the brick and scores points. Clear all bricks to complete the level. Miss the ball and you lose a life.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Mouse or arrow keys to move the paddle
- **Mobile:** Tap and drag to position the paddle

## Tips

The angle the ball bounces depends on where it hits the paddle. Hitting with the edge sends the ball at a sharper angle, which is useful for reaching bricks in tight spots. Keep the paddle centered when possible so you can react to either side.
