Hangman is a word-guessing game. A hidden word is represented by blank spaces. You guess one letter at a time. Correct guesses reveal the letter in its position. Wrong guesses add a body part to the hangman drawing. The game ends when you complete the word or the drawing finishes.

## How it works

A random word is selected and displayed as a series of dashes. Choose a letter. If it appears in the word, all instances are revealed. If it does not, one segment of the hangman figure is drawn. You typically get 6 wrong guesses before the figure is complete and the game is over.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Press a letter key on your keyboard or click the on-screen keyboard.
- **Mobile:** Tap a letter on the on-screen keyboard.

## Strategy/Tips

- Start with common vowels (E, A, I, O) and high-frequency consonants (T, N, S, R). These appear in the most English words.
- Pay attention to word length and revealed letter positions. They narrow down the possibilities quickly.
- Avoid guessing uncommon letters (Q, Z, X) unless the pattern strongly suggests them.
