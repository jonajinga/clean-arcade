2048 is a single-player sliding puzzle played on a 4x4 grid. Each turn, you slide all tiles in one direction. Tiles with the same number merge into one tile with their sum. The goal is to create a tile with the value 2048.

## How it works

After every move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in an empty spot. If no moves are available and the board is full, the game ends. Score is the sum of all merged tiles.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Arrow keys
- **Mobile:** Swipe in any direction

## Strategy

Work from a corner. Keep your highest tile in one corner and build toward it. Avoid pushing your large tile out of position. The game is deterministic in how tiles merge, so plan two or three moves ahead when possible.
