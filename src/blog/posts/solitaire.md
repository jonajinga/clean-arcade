Klondike solitaire is the most widely known version of solitaire. The goal is to move all 52 cards to four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

## How it works

Seven columns of cards are dealt on the tableau. Only the top card of each column is face-up. Cards can be stacked in descending order, alternating colors (red on black, black on red). When an Ace is available, it goes to a foundation pile. Cards from the stock pile are drawn when no moves remain on the tableau.

## Controls

Click and drag cards to move them. Click the stock pile to draw new cards.

## Strategy

Always play Aces and Twos to the foundation immediately — there's no reason to hold them. Expose face-down cards whenever possible. Avoid emptying a tableau column unless you have a King ready to fill it. Draw from the stock pile before making moves on the tableau so you have the full picture.
