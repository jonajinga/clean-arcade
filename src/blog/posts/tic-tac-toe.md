Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players alternate placing X or O. The first to get three in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins.

## How it works

Player X goes first. Each turn, a player places their mark in an empty cell. If all nine cells are filled with no three in a row, the game is a draw.

## Controls

Click or tap any empty cell to place your mark.

## Strategy

Take the center if you go first — it's part of the most winning combinations. If your opponent takes the center, take a corner. With optimal play from both sides, Tic-Tac-Toe always ends in a draw. The game is more about avoiding mistakes than forcing wins. Watch for forks — positions where you threaten two lines at once.
