Color Match is a cognitive reflex game based on the Stroop effect. A color name appears on screen printed in a random ink color. You decide whether the word matches the ink color or not. Each round lasts 30 seconds.

## How it works

A word like "RED" appears in either matching or mismatching ink. You press the correct button to indicate match or mismatch. Correct answers add to your score. Wrong answers cost time or points. The game ends after 30 seconds and displays your final score.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Click the Match or Mismatch button, or use keyboard shortcuts.
- **Mobile:** Tap the on-screen buttons.

## Strategy/Tips

- Read the ink color, not the word. Your brain defaults to reading text, so train yourself to focus on the color instead.
- Go fast but stay accurate. One wrong answer can offset several correct ones.
- If you hesitate, skip and move on. Speed matters in a timed round.
