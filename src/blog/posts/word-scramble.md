Word Scramble presents you with a set of jumbled letters. Your job is to rearrange them into a valid English word before the timer expires.

## How it works

Each round shows a scrambled word. Type or tap letters in the correct order to spell the word. Solve it before time runs out to score points and move to the next round. Words get longer and less common as you progress.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Type letters on your keyboard
- **Mobile:** Tap the letter tiles in order

## Tips

Look for common letter patterns first — prefixes like "un-", "re-", or "pre-" and suffixes like "-tion", "-ing", or "-ment". Identify vowels and consonants early to narrow down possibilities. If you're stuck, try rearranging just the first two or three letters — the rest often falls into place.
