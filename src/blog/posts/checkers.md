Checkers is a two-player board game played on an 8x8 grid. Each player starts with 12 pieces. The objective is to capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them so they can't move.

## How it works

Pieces move diagonally forward one square at a time. To capture, jump over an adjacent opponent piece into an empty square beyond it. Multiple jumps in a single turn are allowed. When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a king and can move diagonally in any direction.

## Controls

Click a piece to select it, then click a valid destination square. Available moves are highlighted.

## Strategy

Control the center of the board. Pieces in the center have more movement options than pieces on the edges. Avoid advancing pieces too quickly — an isolated piece in enemy territory is easy to capture. Try to set up double or triple jumps.
