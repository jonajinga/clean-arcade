Flappy Bird is a side-scrolling game where you keep a bird airborne by tapping to flap its wings. Pipes scroll toward you with gaps at varying heights. Your score equals the number of pipe pairs you pass through.

## How it works

The bird falls due to gravity. Each tap gives it a short upward boost. Pipes appear at regular intervals with a gap between the top and bottom sections. Hitting a pipe or the ground ends the game. Your score increments by one for each pipe pair you clear.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Press Space or click to flap.
- **Mobile:** Tap the screen to flap.

## Strategy/Tips

- Tap in short, consistent intervals rather than panic-mashing. Smooth inputs keep the bird at a manageable height.
- Focus on the upcoming gap, not the bird itself. Anticipate where you need to be.
- Stay near the vertical center of the screen when possible. It gives you room to adjust in either direction.
