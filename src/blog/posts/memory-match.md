Memory Match is a card-matching game on a 4x4 grid. Sixteen cards are placed face down, each with a hidden symbol. Flip two cards per turn. If they match, they stay revealed. If not, they flip back. The game tracks your total moves and completion time.

## How it works

At the start, all 16 cards are face down. Click or tap to flip a card and reveal its symbol. Then flip a second card. If both symbols match, the pair stays face up. If they differ, both flip back after a short delay. The game ends when all 8 pairs are found.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Click a card to flip it.
- **Mobile:** Tap a card to flip it.

## Strategy/Tips

- On your first few turns, focus on memorizing positions rather than hoping for lucky matches.
- Work through the grid systematically. Flip cards in a consistent order so you build a mental map.
- When you know where a match is, clear it immediately rather than exploring more cards. Fewer moves means a better score.
