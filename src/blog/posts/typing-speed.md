Typing Speed is a straightforward typing test. A passage of text appears on screen and you type it as quickly and accurately as possible. After 60 seconds, the game reports your words per minute and accuracy percentage. This game is desktop only.

## How it works

A block of text is displayed at the top of the screen. As you type, correct characters are highlighted green and mistakes are highlighted red. The timer starts when you type your first character. After 60 seconds, the test ends and shows your WPM (total correct words divided by time) and accuracy (correct keystrokes divided by total keystrokes).

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Type on your keyboard. No mouse interaction needed during the test.
- **Mobile:** Not supported. A physical keyboard is required.

## Strategy/Tips

- Accuracy matters more than speed. Correcting errors costs more time than typing slightly slower.
- Keep your eyes on the source text, not on what you have typed. This reduces the urge to backtrack.
- Practice consistently. WPM improves steadily with regular sessions, even short ones.
