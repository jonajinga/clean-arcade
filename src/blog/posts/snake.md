Snake is an arcade game where you control a snake that moves continuously across the screen. Eating food makes the snake grow longer. The game ends when the snake collides with a wall or its own body.

## How it works

The snake moves in one direction until you change it. Each piece of food eaten adds a segment to the tail and increases your score. As the snake grows, the available space shrinks and the game becomes harder.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Arrow keys or WASD
- **Mobile:** Swipe in the direction you want to turn

## Strategy

Stay near the edges early on when the snake is short — there's plenty of room to maneuver. As the snake grows, start moving in tighter patterns to keep open paths available. Avoid trapping yourself in a corner with no way out. Plan your route to the food rather than reacting at the last moment.
