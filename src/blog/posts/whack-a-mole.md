Whack-a-Mole is a reaction-time game played on a 3x3 grid. Moles appear in random holes for a short window. Click or tap them before they disappear to score a point. Each round lasts 30 seconds.

## How it works

Moles pop up one or two at a time in random positions. They stay visible for a brief moment before ducking back down. Successfully clicking a mole adds to your score. As the round progresses, moles appear and disappear faster. Your final score shows when the timer runs out.

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Click on a mole to whack it.
- **Mobile:** Tap on a mole to whack it.

## Strategy/Tips

- Keep your cursor near the center of the grid so you can reach any hole quickly.
- Watch for the animation start rather than waiting until the mole is fully visible. Reacting early gives you more time.
- Stay calm toward the end of the round when speed increases. Rushing leads to misclicks.
