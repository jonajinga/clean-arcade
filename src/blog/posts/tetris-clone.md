This is a falling-block puzzle game in the style of Tetris. Blocks of different shapes fall from the top of the screen. Arrange them to complete horizontal lines, which are then cleared from the board.

## How it works

Seven different block shapes (tetrominoes) appear in random order. Each block falls one row at a time. When a complete horizontal line forms with no gaps, it's removed and everything above shifts down. The game speeds up as you clear more lines. It ends when new blocks can no longer fit on the board.

## Controls

- **Left/Right arrows:** Move block sideways
- **Up arrow:** Rotate block
- **Down arrow:** Soft drop (faster fall)
- **Space:** Hard drop (instant placement)

## Strategy

Keep the board as flat as possible. Avoid creating holes underneath blocks — they're difficult to fill later. Build up one side and leave a column open for the long I-piece to score four lines at once (a "Tetris"). Look at the next piece preview to plan placement before the current piece lands.
